# Premium Maxi Taxi Melbourne Brand Guide

## Brand Position

Premium Maxi Taxi Melbourne is the official public business name for schema, Google Business Profile, accessibility labels, documents, and legal-style references. The visible logo lockup uses the shorter wording Premium Maxi Taxi for cleaner display in the website header, footer, favicon family, and browser surfaces.

Use "maxi cab" as a service and SEO phrase, not as the primary brand name.

## Logo Assets

- `/logo.png` is the primary horizontal logo for light backgrounds, including the header. It uses a realistic black and gold shield badge with a high-roof maxi van and the Premium Maxi Taxi wordmark.
- `/logo-reverse.png` is the horizontal logo for dark backgrounds, including the footer.
- `/logo-mark.png` is the compact square badge for hero, app icon, and social placements.
- `/favicon-32x32.png` is the browser favicon source. It uses the realistic van badge, simplified by scale rather than redrawn as a generic icon.
- `/icon-192.png`, `/icon-512.png`, and `/apple-touch-icon.png` are generated app icon assets.
- `/favicon.ico` is the generated fallback for older browser and platform support.
- `/brand-vehicle-mark.webp` is a supporting visual asset only. Do not embed it in logo, favicon, or app icon SVGs.

Do not rebuild the logo from separate text and icon elements in page templates. Use the prepared raster logo files so spacing, color, and proportions remain consistent. Do not use generated text, fake plates, fake certification badges, fake reviews, or invented operational claims in logo artwork. The supporting website imagery should stay realistic, clean, and premium: white or black maxi vans and uncluttered Melbourne/Victoria settings. When a better approved business vehicle photo is supplied, update supporting image assets while keeping filenames stable.

## Colors

- Premium black: `#111111`
- Deep charcoal: `#1A1A1A`
- Taxi gold: `#D4AF37`
- Light gold highlight: `#F3D878`
- Dark gold shadow: `#A87C1B`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Soft page background: `#F8F9FA`

Black, white, and gold are the core brand colors. Use gold for identity, calls to action, and small emphasis. Avoid making entire sections gold-heavy because it weakens contrast and premium feel.

## Typography

- Headings and logo-supporting display text: Outfit.
- Body, navigation, and functional UI text: Inter.

Keep text sizing stable and responsive by layout constraints, not viewport-width font scaling.

## Clear Space

Leave clear space around the horizontal logo equal to at least the height of the small roof sign inside the logo mark. Do not place the logo tightly against card edges, photos, nav items, or buttons.

For the compact mark, leave at least 12.5% of the mark width as empty space around it when used inside app icons, tiles, or social profiles.

## Trust Rules

Do not add fake addresses, reviews, star ratings, fleet counts, response-time guarantees, licences, ABN, or operating hours until verified business details are supplied. Branding should make the business look professional without inventing operational claims.
